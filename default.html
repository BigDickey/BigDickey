Northeast Phoenix and Scottsdale Area Real Estate Information

The Northeast Phoenix Area ranges from about 1,000 to 1,500 feet higher than the floor of the valley(downtown Phoenix). Nestled in the foothills of the high Sonoran Desert, you can enjoy cooler temperatures, mountain and city light views, and a minimally disturbed natural desert environment.

Looking for a Vacation Home? North Scottsdale is the perfect place to enjoy the luxurious lifestyle offered by this area of the Valley of the Sun.

North East Valley School Information

Cave Creek Homes are in the Cave Creek Unified School District  - The Entire District
North Scottsdale Homes are in the Scottsdale Unified School District  -  Chapparal and Desert Mountain Districts
North Phoenix Homes & Anthem Homes are in the Deer Valley Unified School District  - North Eastern Districts

Northeast Valley Areas

North Scottsdale -- Those areas north of Bell Road and east of Tatum Blvd.
North Phoenix -- Those areas North of the 101 freeway, East of the I-17 west of Tatum Blvd.
Anthem -- On the I-17, three miles North of the Carefree Highway.
Cave Creek -- The Town of Cave Creek.
Carefree -- The Town of Carefree.
